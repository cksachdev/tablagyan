Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can beginners join TablaGyan?

Yes! We offer classes for absolute beginners, intermediate, and advanced learners.

Yes, we conduct both offline and online sessions for students across the globe.

3. Do you teach anything apart from Tabla?

Yes, we also offer vocal music classes for beginners to advanced levels.

4. How do I enroll?

Visit our Contact page and fill out the form to get started. We'll guide you through the process.

5. What is the fee structure?

Fees depend on the level and frequency of classes. Please contact us for detailed information.
