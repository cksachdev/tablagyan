Preserving Tradition, Inspiring Rhythm

TablaGyan is not just a school—it's a sanctuary for rhythm seekers. Founded by passionate percussionists, we focus on developing a deep-rooted understanding of tabla as both a solo and accompanying instrument. Our approach blends discipline with creative freedom, guiding students through the nuances of Indian classical music.

Whether you're an aspiring beginner or an advanced student aiming to refine your technique, our customized classes, jam sessions, and performance opportunities provide a holistic experience. We also emphasize the cultural context of tabla — exploring its role in Kathak, Bhajans, Ghazals, and even modern fusions.

Join a community that values tradition, expression, and the joy of shared rhythm. At TablaGyan, we don’t just play beats — we live them.

TablaGyan Gurus

About Dilip Puranik

The founder, architect, and teacher of TablaGyan – Dilip Puranik has been deeply devoted to music from a very young age. Having played tabla for over 25 years, his journey reflects dedication, passion, and a strong lineage of musical heritage.

Born into a family of musicians, with his father Shri Ramesh Puranik (harmonium player) and mother Smt. Kasturi Puranik (natural singer), Dilip Puranik grew up immersed in music. With the blessings of elders and the grace of Goddess Saraswathi, he has carved a distinct identity as both a performer and a teacher.

Gurus and Lineage

  • Initial Taleem under Pandit Prahlad Raj Mokashi (Dharwad) - Delhi & Banaras Gharanas
  • Advanced Taleem under Pandit Laxmeesh Rao (Sirsi), a Gurubandhu of Ustad Zakir Hussain
  • Specialised training in Punjabi Gharana from senior disciples of Ustad Alla Rakha (Abbaji, father of Ustad Zakir Hussain)

Achievements

  • National Awardee - 2013 for his contribution to music
  • Over 2 decades of active performances and teaching

Performance Experience

  • Accompanied top vocalists and instrumentalists: flute, sitar, and others
  • Versatile in accompanying Classical Music, Light Music, Ghazals, Fusion and more

Vision Behind TablaGyan

TablaGyan is the realization of Dilip Puranik’s dream to nurture musical talent in young minds. For over a decade, he has grown this academy with care, passion, and dedication. His vision is to contribute more towards the art of music, fostering both tradition and innovation through education.

TablaGyan Family

Highlights of Being a Part of TablaGyan Family

  • Music-based coaching in Tabla, Vocal & Harmonium.
