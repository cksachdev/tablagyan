Welcome to TablaGyan

TablaGyan - School of Tabla is a unique music school based in Bangalore, which focuses on imparting the knowledge of Music in general and Tabla in particular. The school is a decade old now and has been a guiding light to many "musically hungry" students. The school has trained more than 150 students now, and currently has a strength of 40+. TablaGyan has not only catered to the musical needs of the locals, but also the foreign nationals, of which most are grooming into quality players.
