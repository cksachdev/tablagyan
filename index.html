About Us

About TablaGyan

TablaGyan - School of Tabla is a unique music school based in Bangalore, which focuses on imparting the knowledge of Music in general and Tabla in particular. The school is a decade old now and has been a guiding light to many "musically hungry" students.

The school has trained more than 150 students now, and currently has a strength of 40+. TablaGyan has not only catered to the musical needs of the locals, but also the foreign nationals, of which most are grooming into quality players.

Courses Offered

Beginner Tabla

Introduction to rhythms, basic bols, hand techniques, and practice routines.

Intermediate Tabla

Taals, improvisation, kaidas, accompaniment, and traditional compositions.

Advanced Tabla

Solo performances, advanced taals, stage presence, and mentorship training.
