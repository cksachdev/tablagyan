About Us

About TablaGyan

Broadly, TablaGyan is a school dedicated to gratify the desires of music lovers. This website is a cautiously nurtured infant of its Tabla Vidyalay at Bengaluru, India.

Though the word "TablaGyan" literally means "Knowledge of Tabla", it figuratively facilitates:

  • Learning and sharing music
  • Being part of a musically active group
  • Allowing rasikas to contribute significantly to the musical world

The school is located in the serene, green lanes of Koramangala, Bengaluru ‚Äî an environment that truly supports musical growth.

Since its inception in 2001, TablaGyan has trained more than 200 students across all age groups, including foreign nationals from the USA, Germany, South Africa, and beyond. Many of these students are now blossoming into brilliant performers.

Courses Offered

Tabla

Beginner Tabla

Basics of rhythm and hand practice.

Click to know more

Core Skills Covered:
  • Understanding basic rhythm cycles (Taal).
  • Correct hand positioning and posture.
  • Mastering fundamental bols: Na, Ta, Tin, Dha, Dhin, etc.
  • Clarity and consistency in sound production.
Focus Areas:
  • Kayda, Paltas (simple patterns).
  • Practice of Teen Taal (16 beats) in slow and medium tempo.
  • Basic accompaniment techniques for Bhajans and light music.
Practice Methods:
  • Repetition exercises for muscle memory.
  • Metronome practice for rhythm consistency.
  • Introduction to practice with Tanpura / Lehra app.
Outcomes After Completion:
  • Ability to play structured patterns independently.
  • Confidence in performing simple compositions on stage.
  • Preparedness to move to Intermediate Tabla training.
