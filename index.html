About Us

About TablaGyan

TablaGyan is a premier institute dedicated to the art of tabla. Founded with a passion for rhythm and tradition, our school focuses on cultivating the next generation of percussionists by blending classical techniques with modern teaching methods.

With personalized instruction, live practice sessions, and a strong focus on Indian classical foundations, TablaGyan nurtures both beginners and advanced students. We host regular workshops, performances, and collaborative events to keep the spirit of rhythm alive.

Courses Offered

Beginner Tabla

Introduction to rhythms, basic bols, hand techniques, and practice routines.

Intermediate Tabla

Taals, improvisation, kaidas, accompaniment, and traditional compositions.

Advanced Tabla

Solo performances, advanced taals, stage presence, and mentorship training.
