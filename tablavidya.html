Tabla Ustaads

The art of tabla has been shaped by generations of Ustaads (Maestros) whose mastery continues to inspire. This section is dedicated to honoring their legacy.

Icons of Tabla

  • Ustad Alla Rakha – Known for bringing tabla to the global stage through collaborations and performances.
  • Ustad Zakir Hussain – A living legend, celebrated for his versatility, innovation, and sheer brilliance.
  • Pandit Kishan Maharaj – Known for mastery over complex compositions and impeccable sense of rhythm.
  • Pandit Samta Prasad – Revered for powerful, dynamic tabla playing rooted in Benares Gharana.
  • Ustad Shafaat Ahmed Khan – Known for melodic approach and finesse in accompaniment.

Their Contributions

  • Elevating tabla from accompaniment to a solo performing art.
  • Spreading Indian percussion globally through performances and collaborations.
  • Mentoring the next generation of percussionists.
  • Innovating within the traditions of different gharanas.

Tabla Ustaads remind us that behind every beat is a legacy of discipline, creativity, and devotion. Their art continues to shape the future of tabla.
