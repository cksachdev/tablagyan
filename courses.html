Our Courses

Tabla Courses

Beginner Tabla

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of tabla playing. Learn the basics of rhythm, hand techniques, and foundational bols (syllables) to build a strong base in Indian percussion.

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Focus: Hand positioning, basic rhythms (e.g., Teen Taal), simple compositions
  • Target: Beginners with no prior experience
  • Mode: In-person or online via Skype
  • Certification: Affiliated with Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva MahaVidyalaya Mandal
Enroll Now

Intermediate Tabla

Build on your foundational skills with various taals and improvisation techniques. Enhance your ability to play complex rhythms and accompany other instruments.

  • Duration: 9 months
  • Focus: Multiple taals (e.g., Jhaptaal, Rupak), improvisation, accompaniment
  • Target: Students with basic tabla knowledge
  • Mode: In-person or online via Skype
  • Certification: Affiliated with Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva MahaVidyalaya Mandal
Enroll Now

Advanced Tabla

Designed for seasoned players, this course focuses on stage performance, advanced taals, and intricate compositions for solo and accompaniment roles.

  • Duration: 12 months
  • Focus: Advanced taals (e.g., Ektaal, Ada Chautaal), solo performances, fusion
  • Target: Experienced tabla players
  • Mode: In-person or online via Skype
  • Certification: Affiliated with Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva MahaVidyalaya Mandal

  • Target: Beginners with no prior vocal experience
  • Mode: In-person or online via Skype
